August 2014   -   Present


Letterly is a side project started by me and my girlfriend. We missed the feeling of receiving handwritten cards from friends and family, so set out to build a service that let anyone send handwritten cards from the comfort of their keyboard. We developed a simple e-commerce website that let users pick one of our illustrated cards, input a message, and click send. Within a few days, the recipient would receive a real handwritten card in the mail (written either by us or one of our expert letterwriters on staff). It's been a wonderful experiment in product and web design, customer acquisition tactics, and building something meaningful from scratch!
